Tuna Pasta Salad

Servings: 5
Prep time - 15 minutes
Cook time - 1 hour

Ingredients:
8 oz - Rotini
2 cans (drained) - Tuna
4 oz - Mozzarella Cheese
1 - Tomato
0.25 - Red Onion
0.5 oz - Olive Oil
1 oz - Lemon Juice
.75 cups - Pesto Sauce

Directions:
Cook Pasta
Rinse pasta with cold water
Drain
Set aside

In medium bowl, combnine all other ingredients (chopped to same size)
Add capers if desired (recommended)
Toss ixture with pasta
chill in fridge 1 hour
enjoy cold!
